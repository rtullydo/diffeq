Step 3: Do Partial Fractions and complete the square:

\begin{equation*} \frac{2}{\left(s-1\right)\left(s^{2}-4s+5\right)}=\frac{A}{s-1}+\frac{Bs+C}{s^{2}-4s+5} \end{equation*}

and get \(A=1,B=-1,C=3\) so that

\begin{equation*} \frac{2}{\left(s-1\right)\left(s^{2}-4s+5\right)}=\frac{1}{s-1}+\frac{-s+3}{s^{2}-4s+5} \end{equation*}
