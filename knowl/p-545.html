Generally, a transform of a function \(f(t)\) turns \(f(t)\)m> into a different function with potentially more tractible properties. We will transform functions \(f(t)\) of \(t\) in the time domain into functions \(F(s)\) of \(s\) in the frequency domain. The use of these terms is deliberate and will become clear on further study.
