Theorem 3.3.3. Superposition of solutions to linear homogeneous ODE.

If \(y_{1}\) and \(y_{2}\) are two solutions to an ODE

\begin{equation*} y^{\prime\prime}+p(t)y^{\prime}+q(t)y=0, \end{equation*}

then the linear combination \(y(t)=c_{1}y_{1}(t)+c_{2}y_{2}(t)\) is also a solution for any values \(c_{1},c_{2}\text{.}\)
