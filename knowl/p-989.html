Consider the following undamped harmonic oscillator with external forcing:

\begin{equation*} u^{\prime\prime}+5u=\sin\left(3t\right),\,\,\,\,u(0)=1,\,\,u^{\prime}(0)=-1. \end{equation*}

What is the natural frequency? What is the frequency for the external force? Will you get resonance? What is your guess for \(u_{p}\text{?}\) Solve the IVP.
