Step 3: Do Partial Fractions and complete the square:

\begin{equation*} \frac{3s}{\left(s^{2}+4\right)\left(s^{2}+1\right)}=\frac{As+B}{s^{2}+4}+\frac{Cs+C}{s^{2}+1} \end{equation*}

and get \(A=-1,B=0,C=1,D=0\) so that

\begin{equation*} \frac{3s}{\left(s^{2}+4\right)\left(s^{2}+1\right)}=\frac{-s}{s^{2}+4}+\frac{s}{s^{2}+1} \end{equation*}
