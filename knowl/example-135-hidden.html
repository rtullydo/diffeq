Find the Laplace Transform of

\begin{equation*} f(t)=\begin{cases} 2 \amp t\lt3\\ -3 \amp t\geq3 \end{cases}. \end{equation*}

Solution: First use the technique from the first two examples two write \(f(t)\) in terms of \(u_{c}\text{,}\) and get

\begin{equation*} f(t)=2-5u_{3}(t), \end{equation*}

hence

\begin{equation*} F(s)=\mathcal{L}\left\{ f(t)\right\} =\frac{2}{s}-5\frac{e^{-3s}}{s}. \end{equation*}
