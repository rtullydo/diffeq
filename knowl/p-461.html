Step1: Recall that \(r^{2}+2=0\) so \(r=\pm\sqrt{2}i\text{,}\) so that

\begin{equation*} u_{h}(t)=c_{1}\cos\left(\sqrt{2}t\right)+c_{2}\sin\left(\sqrt{2}t\right). \end{equation*}

The natural frequency of the system is \(\omega_{0}=\sqrt{2}\text{.}\) The external frequency is \(\omega=\sqrt{2}\text{.}\) Since they match, then we will get resonance!
