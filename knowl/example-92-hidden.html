Are the following functions piecewise continutions?

\begin{equation*} f(t)=\begin{cases} t^{2} \amp 0\leq t\leq1\\ 1 \amp 1\lt t\leq2\\ 4-t \amp 2\lt t\leq3 \end{cases} \end{equation*}

and

\begin{equation*} g(t)=\begin{cases} t^{2} \amp 0\leq t\leq1\\ (t-1)^{-1} \amp 1\lt t\leq2\\ 1 \amp 2\lt t\leq3. \end{cases} \end{equation*}

Solution: Sketch the graphs \(f(t)\) is piecewise continuous since it only has a jump discontinuity. \(g(t)\) is not since it has a discontinuity that is not jump or removable.
