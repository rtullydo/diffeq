An initial value problem (IVP) consists of a system of differential equations and intial conditions such as

\begin{equation*} \begin{cases} x_{1}^{\prime}=a(t)x_{1}+b(t)x_{2} \amp x_{1}(t_{0})=x_{1}^{0}\\ x_{2}^{\prime}=c(t)x_{1}+d(t)x_{2} \amp x_{2}(t_{0})=x_{2}^{0} \end{cases} \end{equation*}
