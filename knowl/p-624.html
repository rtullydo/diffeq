We look at the critical points which are \(t=2,4\) and consider different cases: \(t\lt2\text{,}\) \(f(t)=0+0=0\) \(2\leq t\lt4\text{,}\) \(f(t)=2t\cdot1+0=2t\text{,}\) \(4\leq t\text{,}\) \(f(t)=2t\cdot1-(t-1)\cdot1=t+1\text{,}\) hence

\begin{equation*} f(t)=\begin{cases} 0 \amp t\lt 2\\ 2t \amp 2\leq t \lt 4\\ t+1 \amp t\geq4. \end{cases} \end{equation*}
