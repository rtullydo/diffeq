Solution: Let \(R(v)=v^{2}/5\) be the resistance, then

\begin{equation*} m\frac{dv}{dt}=-R(v)-mg, \end{equation*}

and we have \(-R(v)\) since the object is still going up. Thus

\begin{equation*} m\frac{dv}{dt}=-\frac{v^{2}}{5}-mg\iff m\frac{dv}{dt}=-\frac{v^{2}}{5}-98 \end{equation*}
