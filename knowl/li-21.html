A Linear Differential Equation: Pick your favorite real numbers \(a,b,y_{0}\) and consider the IVP

\begin{equation*} \frac{dy}{dt}=ay-b,\,\,\,\,\,\,y(0)=y_{0}. \end{equation*}

The general solution to this differential equation is

\begin{equation*} y(t)=\frac{b}{a}+\left(y_{0}-\frac{b}{a}\right)e^{at} \end{equation*}
