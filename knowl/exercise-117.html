Exercise 2.

Consider the following system

\begin{equation*} \mathbf{x}^{\prime}=\left(\begin{array}{cc} -2 \amp -1\\ 1 \amp -4 \end{array}\right)\mathbf{x} \end{equation*}

  1. Find the general solution

  2. Solve the IVP

    \begin{equation*} \mathbf{x}^{\prime}=\left(\begin{array}{cc} -2 \amp -1\\ 1 \amp -4 \end{array}\right)\mathbf{x},\,\,\,\,\,\mathbf{x}(0)=\left(\begin{array}{c} -1\\ 3 \end{array}\right) \end{equation*}

  3. Determine the direction of the oscillations in the phase plane (do solutions go clockwise or counterclockwise)

  4. Classify the Equilibrium solution

  5. Draw the Phase Portrait
