Exercise 2.

Find the general solution to

\begin{equation*} t^{2}y^{\prime\prime}-4ty^{\prime}+6y=t^{3},\,\,\,\,t>0 \end{equation*}

given that

\begin{equation*} y_{1}(t)=t^{2},\,\,\,y_{2}(t)=t^{3} \end{equation*}

forms a fundamental set of solution for the corresponding homogeneous differential equation.
