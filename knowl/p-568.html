The Inverse Laplace transform \(\mathcal{L}^{-1}\) is the function that satisfies \(\mathcal{L}^{-1}\left\{ \mathcal{L}\left[f\right]\right\} =f\text{.}\) In other words,

\begin{equation*} \mathcal{L}^{-1}\left\{ F\right\} =f\iff\mathcal{L}\left\{ f\right\} =F. \end{equation*}
