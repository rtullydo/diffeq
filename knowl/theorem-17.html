Theorem 6.7.1.

The Laplace transform of the convolution is

\begin{equation*} \mathcal{L}\left\{ \left(f\star g\right)(t)\right\} =\mathcal{L}\left\{ f(t)\right\} \mathcal{L}\left\{ g(t)\right\} =F(s)G(s); \end{equation*}

that is

\begin{equation*} \mathcal{L}^{-1}\left\{ F(s)G(s)\right\} =\left(f\star g\right)(t)=\int_{0}^{t}f\left(t-\tau\right)g\left(\tau\right)d\tau. \end{equation*}
