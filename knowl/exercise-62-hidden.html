Consider the following non-homogeneous 2nd order ODE:
\begin{equation*} y^{\prime\prime}+y^{\prime}-2y=e^{3t}. \end{equation*}
  1. Find the general solution.
  2. Find the particular solution to the IVP:
    \begin{equation*} y^{\prime\prime}+y^{\prime}-2y=e^{3t},\,\,\,\,y(0)=\frac{1}{10},y^{\prime}(0)=\frac{13}{10}. \end{equation*}
