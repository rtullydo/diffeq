Exercise 2.

A \(32\) lb mass stretches a spring 4 feet. The mass is displaced an additional 6 feet. and then released with an initial velocity of 3 \(\frac{\text{ft}}{\text{sec}}\text{;}\) and is in a medium with a damping coefficients \(\gamma=2\frac{\text{lb sec}}{\text{ft}}\text{.}\) Suppose there is an external forcing due to wind given by \(F(t)=3\cos\left(3t\right)\text{.}\) Formulate the IVP that governs the motion of this mass.
