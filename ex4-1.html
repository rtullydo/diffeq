Exercises Exercises

1.

What is the largest interval for which there exists a unique solution by the Existence and Uniqueness Theorem for the following IVP:

\begin{equation*} \begin{cases} \left(t-5\right)y^{(4)}-\frac{\ln\left(t+7\right)}{t}y^{\prime\prime}+e^{t}y=\frac{t^{2}+1}{\left(t-1\right)}\\ y(2)=-1\\ y^{\prime}(2)=1\\ y^{\prime\prime}(2)=2\\ y^{\prime\prime\prime}(2)=5. \end{cases} \end{equation*}
2.

Find general solution of

\begin{equation*} y^{\prime\prime\prime}+10y^{\prime\prime}+7y^{\prime}-18y=0. \end{equation*}

(Hint: \(r^{3}+10r^{2}+7r-18=\left(r-1\right)\left(r+2\right)\left(r+9\right)\))

3.

Find general solution of

\begin{equation*} y^{(4)}-10y^{\prime\prime\prime}+36y^{\prime\prime}-54y^{\prime}+27y=0. \end{equation*}

(Hint: \(r^{4}-10r^{3}+36r^{2}-54r+27=\left(r-1\right)\left(r-3\right)^{3}\))

4.

Find general solution of

\begin{equation*} y^{(5)}-4y^{(4)}+13y^{\prime\prime\prime}-36y^{\prime\prime}+36y=0. \end{equation*}

(Hint: \(r^{5}-4r^{4}+13r^{3}-36r^{2}+36r=r\left(r-2\right)^{2}\left(r^{2}+9\right)\))

5.

Find general solution of

\begin{equation*} y^{(4)}+11y^{\prime\prime}+18y=0. \end{equation*}

(Hint: \(r^{4}+11r^{2}+18=\left(r^{2}+2\right)\left(r^{2}+9\right)\))

6.

Find general solution of

\begin{equation*} y^{(6)}+32y^{(4)}+256y^{\prime\prime}=0. \end{equation*}

(Hint: \(r^{6}+32r^{4}+256r^{2}=r^{2}\left(r^{2}+16\right)^{2}\))
